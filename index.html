A comfortable and relaxed meal could be a catalyst in achieving a better outcome one day at a time. To ensure the same we've come up with this project wherein our website could be efficient in choosing menu, ordering food, drinks and effortless payment and accordingly the bill generated could be verified with a QR code scanner at the providers end and makes the entire food ordering process less time consuming.


1. To ensure easy access to the menu without having to wait or rush in- and -out of the canteen .

2. ‌The food menu would be updated by the canteen staff .

3.‌ To order food via the website and generate a QR code for the provider to refer the order .‌

4. Effective payment process once the order is generated by the consumer and received at the providers side .‌

5. To ensure reliable access to the canteen menu from any distance and to be able to order and receive food without a hustle in the canteen.
